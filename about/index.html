Our Story

Studio Raama began with a shared belief that the spaces we inhabit should feel as considered as they are comfortable.

Founded by Aman and Radhika, the studio grew from a shared passion for design, where functionality and aesthetics are given equal importance.

What started as an instinct to create thoughtful, well-balanced spaces has evolved into a practice rooted in understanding people and the way they live.

Today, we continue to shape homes and commercial environments with a quiet attention to detail, an intuitive approach to layouts and materials, and a commitment to creating spaces that feel effortless, personal, and enduring.

meet the duo behind it all

Co-Founder & Principal Designer

Radhika Maheshwari

Radhika brings a sensitive and intuitive approach to design, focusing on how spaces feel as much as how they look. She is drawn to the quiet beauty of textures, while also finding joy in working with pretty, bold colours and thoughtfully curated palettes. With an intuitive, detail-driven approach, she enjoys planning layouts, layering furniture, and bringing spaces to life through styling and staging. Her work results in homes that feel warm, balanced, and deeply personal.

Co-Founder & Principal Designer

Aman Tank

Aman is driven by a passion for creating spaces that feel both refined and deeply personal. With a strong eye for detail and a focus on functionality, he approaches design as a balance between aesthetics and everyday living. His style leans towards clean, thoughtful compositions enhanced by natural materials and subtle textures. Aman believes that good design should not only look beautiful but also feel intuitive, comfortable, and timeless.
