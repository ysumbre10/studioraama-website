About Us

A boutique interior design studio in Pune, founded on the belief that great design should feel personal.

Our Story

Studio Raama is a boutique interior design studio dedicated to crafting spaces that reflect your personality, lifestyle, and aspirations. We believe that great design goes beyond aesthetics — it should feel personal, functional, and deeply connected to the people who inhabit the space.

Founded by Aman and Radhika, Studio Raama brings together modern design sensibilities with thoughtful functionality. Our approach focuses on understanding each client's vision and translating it into interiors that are both beautiful and livable.

From concept to completion, we carefully curate materials, textures, and details to create spaces that feel balanced, timeless, and uniquely yours. Whether it's a home, workspace, or commercial environment, our goal is to design interiors that inspire comfort, creativity, and everyday living.

Our Philosophy

We believe every space has a story waiting to be told. Our role is to listen carefully, understand deeply, and design thoughtfully — so that the finished space doesn't just look beautiful, but feels unmistakably like home.

Good design isn't about trends or excess. It's about balance — between aesthetics and function, between bold choices and quiet elegance, between the designer's craft and the client's vision. That balance is at the heart of everything we do.

The Founders

Meet the Team

Aman Tank

Co-Founder & Principal Designer

Aman is driven by a passion for creating spaces that feel both refined and deeply personal. With a strong eye for detail and a focus on functionality, he approaches design as a balance between aesthetics and everyday living. His style leans towards clean, thoughtful compositions enhanced by natural materials and subtle textures. Aman believes that good design should not only look beautiful but also feel intuitive, comfortable, and timeless.

Radhika Maheshwari

Co-Founder & Principal Designer

Radhika brings a sensitive and intuitive approach to design, focusing on how spaces feel as much as how they look. She enjoys crafting environments that are warm, inviting, and layered with meaningful details. With a love for soft palettes, textures, and storytelling through design, her work reflects a sense of calm and harmony. Radhika believes that every space should tell a story — one that resonates with the people who live in it.
