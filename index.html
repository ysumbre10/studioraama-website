Crafting Spaces, Creating Stories

Studio Raama is a boutique interior design studio dedicated to crafting spaces that reflect your personality, lifestyle, and aspirations. We believe that great design goes beyond aesthetics — it should feel personal, functional, and deeply connected to the people who inhabit the space.

Founded by Aman and Radhika, Studio Raama brings together modern design sensibilities with thoughtful functionality. Our approach focuses on understanding each client's vision and translating it into interiors that are both beautiful and livable.

From concept to completion, we carefully curate materials, textures, and details to create spaces that feel balanced, timeless, and uniquely yours. Whether it's a home, workspace, or commercial environment, our goal is to design interiors that inspire comfort, creativity, and everyday living.

What We Do

Our Services

At Studio Raama, we create thoughtful and well-balanced interiors that reflect both functionality and aesthetic sensibility. Our approach combines design creativity with careful planning and execution to bring each space together seamlessly.

Concept & Mood Board

Every project begins with shaping a clear design vision. We understand the client's lifestyle, preferences, and the story they want their home to tell, translating ideas into curated mood boards that establish a cohesive design language.

Space Planning

Good design begins with thoughtful planning. We carefully analyze the layout to ensure efficient function and balanced visual flow, exploring furniture arrangements, circulation paths, and spatial relationships to maximize comfort.

Design Development

Once the layout is finalized, we refine the concept into tangible design elements — furniture layouts, custom features, lighting plans, and spatial compositions. This is where ideas start taking shape and the design truly comes alive.

Material & Finishes Selection

We carefully curate materials such as wood finishes, laminates, fabrics, tiles, stones, and surfaces that align with the design concept, considering texture, durability, color harmony, and overall visual balance.

Site Supervision

We regularly monitor on-site progress, coordinating with contractors, craftsmen, and vendors to maintain design consistency and quality, bridging the gap between design drawings and real-world execution.

Staging & Styling

Through thoughtful placement of furniture, decor, textiles, art, and accessories, we create a cohesive and inviting environment that transforms the space from a finished project into a beautifully curated home.

The Founders

Meet the Team

Aman Tank

Co-Founder & Principal Designer

Aman is driven by a passion for creating spaces that feel both refined and deeply personal. With a strong eye for detail and a focus on functionality, he approaches design as a balance between aesthetics and everyday living. His style leans towards clean, thoughtful compositions enhanced by natural materials and subtle textures. Aman believes that good design should not only look beautiful but also feel intuitive, comfortable, and timeless.

Radhika Maheshwari

Co-Founder & Principal Designer

Radhika brings a sensitive and intuitive approach to design, focusing on how spaces feel as much as how they look. She enjoys crafting environments that are warm, inviting, and layered with meaningful details. With a love for soft palettes, textures, and storytelling through design, her work reflects a sense of calm and harmony. Radhika believes that every space should tell a story — one that resonates with the people who live in it.
